Overview

Purpose

Our website will showcase our handcrafted writing instruments, providing information about our unique pens and the craftsmanship behind them. It will offer an easy shopping experience and a Contact Us page for inquiries. We aim to grow our business and connect with those who appreciate handmade quality.

Scenarios

Question 1: Are you an avid collector or enthusiast of unique, high-quality writing instruments looking for handcrafted pens that stand out from mass-produced options?

Question 2: Do you value traditional craftsmanship and wish to support small businesses that handcraft their products with passion and skill, just as our grandpa did in his shop?
